  • Backgrounds:

    Accumulating scientific and clinical evidences have suggested the use of drug combinations as a safe and effective approach, to treat complicated and refractory diseases. By collecting and organizing known examples of drug combinations, we aim to provide updated comprehensive information of drug combinations and further facilitate to predict the potential useful drugs

    Current Information:

    Currently we have collected 435 drug combinations and 393 individual drugs and 795 targets, and corresponding information. In the near future, the funtion of prediction module and data update module will be provided.
